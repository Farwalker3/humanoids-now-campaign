# Everything Kickstarter Requires (campaign build checklist)

Compiled from Kickstarter's Creator Handbook, Rules, and the 2025 Pre-Launch Checklist. Each item
maps to a section already present in `index.html` (marked ✅) or is an off-page account requirement.

## A. Eligibility (to create a campaign at all)
- Be **18+**.
- Be a resident of a **supported country** with a valid government-issued **ID**.
- Have a **bank account** in that country and a major **debit/credit card**.
- Have an **address, phone number**, and (US) SSN/EIN for tax/identity verification.
- Project must make **something to share with others** and fit an eligible **category**.
- Must **not** be on the Prohibited list (no charity/cause donations, no equity, no multi-level
  marketing, no prohibited items).

## B. Basics (the top of the builder)
- **Project title** ✅
- **Subtitle / short blurb** (≈135 chars) ✅ (`<p class="sub">`)
- **Category & subcategory** ✅ (Robotics · Open Hardware)
- **Project image** — main thumbnail, min 1024×576, no logos/badges ✅ (hero)
- **Project location** ✅ (creator section — set your city)
- **Funding goal** + currency ✅ (`GOAL`)
- **Campaign duration** — 1–60 days (30 recommended) ✅ (`DAYS`)
- **Launch date** (schedule) — set in Kickstarter builder (off-page)

## C. Project video (strongly required in practice)
- A short **intro video** (hook → who you are → what you're making → why → the ask).
- Must show the product's **real, current state** — **no CGI / special effects** implying
  functionality that doesn't exist yet. ✅ (hero has a video slot + honesty pledge; drop your MP4/embed in)

## D. Story (the campaign body)
- **The pitch / story** with text + images/GIFs ✅ (Campaign tab)
- Recommended structure: hook → problem → solution → features → proof/what-exists → budget →
  roadmap → team → why now ✅ (all present)
- **Risks and challenges** — a **required** dedicated section ✅
- **Budget / use of funds** (recommended) ✅
- **FAQ** ✅

## E. Rewards / tiers (each reward needs all of these)
- **Title** ✅ · **Pledge amount** ✅ · **Description** ✅
- **Items included** ✅ · **Estimated delivery** (month/year) ✅
- **Shipping** (destinations + cost, or digital) ✅
- **Quantity / backer limit** (optional) ✅ · time limit (optional)
- Rule: rewards must be **new, your own work**, produced/designed by the project ✅

## F. People
- **Collaborators** (add teammates by KS username) — off-page
- **Verified identity** — off-page

## G. Payment & fees (off-page account setup)
- Connect **bank account** + verify identity.
- **Fees:** Kickstarter **5%** + payment processing (~3–5%) — budget for it ✅ (in budget table)
- **All-or-nothing:** funded only if goal met by deadline; backers charged on success ✅ (stated)

## H. Creator profile
- **Name, bio, photo/avatar, location** ✅ (creator section)
- Links to website / other projects ✅

## I. Optional but expected in 2025
- **Environmental commitments** section (KS prompts for it) — add if applicable
- **Community / backer map**, **Updates** plan ✅ (tabs present)
- A **pre-launch page** + email list before launch (marketing, off-page)

> This page is a faithful mirror of a live Kickstarter campaign layout. To actually launch, create
> the campaign in Kickstarter's builder and paste this story/rewards in, complete B/F/G off-page, and
> replace the placeholder figures. This hosted page works now as your landing/pre-launch page.
